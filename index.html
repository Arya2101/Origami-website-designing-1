  • Interesting facts about origami
  1. Origami is the Japanese art of folding paper.
  2. 'Ori' is the Japanese word for folding.
  3. 'Gami' is the Japanese word for paper.
  4. By adding these 2 words together, origami got its name.
  5. However, origami did not start in Japan, it started in China.
